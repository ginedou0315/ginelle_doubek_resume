Ginelle Doubek

~Ginny~

"I am a woman of action."

At the beginning of my career, I was leaning towards of becoming a researcher. I finished my Bachelors in Agricultural Economics and majored in Price and Marketing analysis. My first job was in Data management for local projects that my previous university was conducting. I was collecting and storing information from each projects assigned to me. I only stayed on the job for one year and started to look for something more challenging. I moved to the city and found a job as a Sales Assistant. Just a month being in the position, my immediate manager saw the potential in me and promoted me to Sales Manager. This started my journey in Sales and Marketing from working with different multinational companies like Unilever, Procter & Gamble and Nestle. After a few years working in Sales, I took break to study Master in Business Management. During this time, I also worked as Research assistant part-time for a non-profit organization (International Rice Research Institute).

I moved to Dubai, UAE 2 years after my career break to try my luck in a new city. I found a Sales job in the hotel industry. I started as a Sales Specialist for Sheraton JBR and after a year of hard work, I was chosen to be part of the Global Sales Office for the Mother company "Starwood Hotels". When my 8 years stint in Dubai has come to an end, I moved to Switzerland with my husband. For another 2 years, I continued working remotely from Switzerland for a local company in Dubai representing them in European countries. I also made use of this time to learn German. I got my first job as a Congress Coordinator for EULAR in Zurich.

In 2023, I found my passion in coding for Web Development. I have completed various course thru online Bootcamp. This experience has ignited my desire to learn and grow in this field. For the past 2 years, I have honed my skills in HTML, CSS and JavaScript. I have created applications and websites. I believe that all these years of my experience have given me different skill set that I could apply to my personal and professional life. I am dedicated and driven, I constantly putting in effort to achieve my goals and improve them. I am always eager to learn and explore new ideas. My optimism and belief in myself give me the strength to tackle any obstacle and pursue my ambitions. "Quitting" is not in my vocabulary. I believe in the saying "When the going gets tough, the tough get going".
